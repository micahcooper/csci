        • Regents Testing
          • If you make 510 Verbal SAT of 23 Reading ACT(or higher) you will exempt the Regents Reading Exam.
          • If you have a 530 Verbal SAT or 23 ACT(or higher) and an in ENGL1101, you will exempt the Regents Essay exam.
          • If you have a 590 Verbal SAT or 26 ACT(or higher) and a B in ENGL1101, you will exempt the Regents Essay exam.
          • Sign up on Oasis during the first two weeks of the semester to take the Regents' exam. You must pass the Essay section by the time you earn 45 hours credit, or within two semesters at UGA, if you have transferred from a non-University System of Georgia college of university, or you will have to take remedial English and reading courses. You are exempt from the Reading section.
